About me

My name is Lucas Briand, I am a student in computer science.

ORIGINS

I come from Epinal where I did a scientific bachelor's degree. But I’ve been living in Nancy for 3 years to study.

Studies

. I studied computer science in Technical College to obtain my 2-year diploma and now I am studying web application development to reach my Vocational bachelor’s degree. I’m currently on a sandwich course alternating between studies and work at Orange, the French telecommunications company.

Hobbies

I'm a big movie fan, I go three times a week. I have also been a musician for 1 year now. I play guitar, I learned on my own and I spend at least 1 hour and half a day training. I like sport too, especially football and basketball.
